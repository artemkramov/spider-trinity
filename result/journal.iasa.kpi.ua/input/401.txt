P. Zaychenko, S. V. Rogoza, V. A. Stolbunov</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div>The problem of analysis bankruptcy risk of enterprises is considered. Altmann’s classical method of discriminate analysis is described, and its highs and lows are analized. Possibility of its application to the Ukrainian economy is estimated. Furthermore, the fuzzy sets approach to bankruptcy risk analysis is described. The application of fuzzy neural networks with different algorithms of fuzzy inference is considered. The results of comparative investigations of different methods for bankruptcy analysis are presented.</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://journal.iasa.kpi.ua/article/view/107360/102318">PDF (Русский)</a></div><div><h4>GOST Style Citations</h4><br><div>—</div></div><br><br><div></div><h3>Refbacks</h3><ul><li>There are currently no refbacks.</li></ul></div></div></div></div>
