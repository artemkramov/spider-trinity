Reutov</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div>Theoretical propositions are considered concerning design of one-rate and multirate systems for forecasting state and measurement vectors under one-rate discretization of all coordinates as well as under discretization of control and disturbance vectors with small sampling periods and of state and measurement vectors with large ones. Forecasting functions are developed by using Diophant equations in matrix polinoms.
