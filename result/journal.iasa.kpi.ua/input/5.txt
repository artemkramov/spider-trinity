mathematical models of information spreading process under uncertainty</h3></div><div><em>Oleksandr Nakonechnyi, Petro Zinko, Iuliia Shevchuk</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div>The mathematical model of spreading the information of an arbitrary number of types was considered. The model takes the form of a system of non-linear ordinary differential equations with stationary parameters. Special cases of presenting observation errors are considered. The algorithms for building averaged optimal rms predictive estimation and guaranteed predictive estimation are offered. The algorithm for building averaged optimal rms predictive estimation for a case of spreading information of one type and the algorithm for finding guaranteed predictive estimation for a particular case of representing a set of possible observation errors are obtained. The results of a numerical experiment for the problem of building guaranteed predictive estimates for the system with two sources of information are considered.</div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>models of information spreading process; predictive estimation; uncertainty; rms estimation</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://journal.iasa.kpi.ua/article/view/111225/114232">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>GOST Style Citations</h4><br><div>—</div></div><br><br><br><br>DOI:<a href="https://doi.org/10.20535/SRIT.2308-8893.2017.4.05">https://doi.org/10.20535/SRIT.2308-8893.2017.4.05</a><div></div><h3>Refbacks</h3><ul><li>There are currently no refbacks.</li></ul></div></div></div></div>
