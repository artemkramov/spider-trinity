It is shown that the recording with the laser assistance has substantial advantages in comparison with another methods. The magnetooptical characteristics of the nanogranular CoPt-Ag films are investigated. The results confirm the possibility of their application for superdensity recording. The magnetoresistive and magnetorefractive effects in the films based on CoFe/Al2O3 have been investigated. A storage mechanism is found in these films. Fig.: 4.
