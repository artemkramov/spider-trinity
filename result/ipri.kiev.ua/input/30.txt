The obtained conditional estimations have a higher exactness by comparison with unconditional ones obtained by methods of classical parametrical evaluation exclusively by registration data. The problem is set and solved in general form so that the methods and results of its solution can be used for the simultaneous estimation of parameters totality of the registration object of any physical nature if connection between the registration data in the form of the finite equations is established a priori.
