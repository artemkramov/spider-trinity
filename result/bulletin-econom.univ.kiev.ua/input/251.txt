Dyachenko, post-graduate Dnipropetrovsk State Financial Academy, Dnipropetrovsk THE STATE FINANCIAL CONTROL IN CONTEXT OF THE STRENGTHENING OF OPERABILITY OF BUDGETARY POLICY OF STIMULATION OF REGIONAL DEVELOPMENT</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/1442" title="01:18 AM">Sunday November 1st, 2015</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/maxim" title="View all posts by Maxim">Maxim</a></span></span></div><div><p>In this article the essence of state financial control as an important factor of strengthening of operability of budgetary policy of stimulation of the socio-economic<span></span>development of regions is disclosed. The estimating index of the level of execution of the budgetary stimulative programs is offered.</p><p>Keywords: state financial control, budgetary mechanism of stimulation of regional development, audit of efficiency, index of execution of efficiency (quality) exponents</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2014/154-1/19">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2014/154-1/19</a></p><h5><strong><span>References&nbsp;</span></strong></h5><ol><li>Pikhotskyi V, 2009.
