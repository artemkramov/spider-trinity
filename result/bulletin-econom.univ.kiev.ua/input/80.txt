Novikova, PhD in Economics, Senior Researcher Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine INSTITUTIONAL ASPECTS OF THE CAUSES AND CONSEQUENCES VIOLATION OF FINANCIAL STABILITY: HISTORICAL RETROSPECTIVE AND CURRENT UKRAINIAN REALITIES IN THE CONTEXT OF CHALLENGES OF EUROPEAN INTEGRATION</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/7695" title="10:45 PM">Friday June 10th, 2016</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/maxim" title="View all posts by Maxim">Maxim</a></span></span></div><div><p>The article analyzes the institutional aspect of the causes and consequences of violations of financial stability. Done analysis of famous historical examples of the emergence of inflationary bursts, as<span></span>well as ways of establishing a financial equilibrium. In particular, states that often main cause of violations of financial stability becomes inflationary boom, which arose by wars, socio-economic and political contradictions. It was considered role of institutional instability in the context of the emergence of contemporary geopolitical challenges and socio-economic changes in Ukraine. The paper examines the impact of modern social and economic challenges on growth in inflation and on the deterioration of other macroeconomic indicators in Ukraine. At the end, was provided of the recommendations to overcome the financial problems in the national economy. Emphasized importance of the exchange rate stability of the currency.</p><p>Keywords: financial stability, market economy, banks, inflation, institutional reforms, European integration.</p><p>Date of submission 15.04.16</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/182-5/4">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/182-5/4</a></p><p><strong>References</strong></p><ol><li>Orlov A.S., Georgiev V.A., Georgieva N.G., Sivohina T.A.
