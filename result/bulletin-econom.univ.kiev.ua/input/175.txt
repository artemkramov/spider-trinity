Kryvko, economist Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine MODERN AREAS OF DEVELOPMENT OF RESOURCE-BASED THEORY</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/1213" title="11:52 AM">Sunday October 18th, 2015</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/admin" title="View all posts by admin">admin</a></span></span></div><div><p>The essence of resource-based view is focus on using unique and rare combination of resources, core competences and organizational capabilities of the firm. The new intellectual resources and their combination<span></span>provide the formation of sustainable competitive advantages. Therefore, it seems there is necessity to join in research the application of resource-based theory to creation of new combination of resources in dynamic environment. In this paper modern areas of development of resource-based theory have been considered.
