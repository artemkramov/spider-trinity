Pimenova, PhD in Economics, Assistant Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine PROSPECTS OF DEVELOPMENT OF SMALL AND MEDIUM-SIZED ENTERPRISES IN UKRAINIAN AGRICULTURE</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/7763" title="07:37 PM">Wednesday July 6th, 2016</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/maxim" title="View all posts by Maxim">Maxim</a></span></span></div><div><p>Main directions of development of small and medium-sized enterprises in Ukrainian agriculture are examined. It is determined that entrepreneurship in agrarian sector is a driving force behind the<span></span>development of agriculture, as these activities contributes to the production of quality agricultural products that satisfies the needs of society, food security, independence and improves the living standards of rural population.</p><p>Potential customers of small parties of harvest in Ukrainian grain market are analyzed. The ways of mutually beneficial cooperation between Ukrainian farmers and entrepreneurs are proved.</p><p>The study of foreign experience has proven that strengthening the competitiveness of Ukrainian agricultural products in the context of globalization is possible on the basis of mutually beneficial cooperation of Ukrainian farmers and entrepreneurs, where recent purchase of output at market prices for the purpose of resale or creation of agricultural cooperatives, such form of economy in agrarian relations that combines a balanced economic, social and environmental components.</p><p>In particular in paper is shown that sustainable economic management provide only forms of economy that take into account the socioeconomic and geophysical specifics of agriculture, particularities of agricultural labor and methods of management. The most common among these forms are farmers.</p><p>Key words: agriculture, entrepreneurship, trade, farmer, grain, customers.</p><p>Date of submission 23.03.16</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/183-6/6">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2016/183-6/6</a></p><p><strong>References</strong></p><ol><li>Sil’s’kegospodarstvoUkraTni (2014) DerzhavnasluzhbastatistikiUkraTni.
