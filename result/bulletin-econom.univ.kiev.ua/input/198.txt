Bilorus, PhD in Economics, Associate Professor Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine METHODOLODICAL TOOLS FOR ORGANIZING THE PROCESS OF STAFF SEARCH AND SELECTION</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/3842" title="11:52 AM">Friday November 27th, 2015</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/admin" title="View all posts by admin">admin</a></span></span></div><div><p>The relevance and the basic stages to meet the demand in personnel in the contemporary economy were determined.<span></span>The necessity and requirements for the formation of maps of competencies as tools for personnel selection were proved and generalized. Practical recommendations of candidates selection procedure for the organization were provided using the method of “ideal point”.</p><p>Keywords: need in personnel, personnel recruitment and selection, map of competencies, sources of candidates search, selection methods, evaluation of candidates for the vacant position.</p><p>DOI:<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/172-7/3">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/172-7/3</a></p><h5><strong>References</strong></h5><ol><li>Dessler, G.
