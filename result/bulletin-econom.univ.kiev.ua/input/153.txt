Studied the basic conditions and<span></span>development trends of the Internet insurance market in the process of informatization of insurance companies. The basic concept of interaction between the subjects of the insurance market through the website were noticed. The study reveals the necessity and expediency of using new approaches of the acquisition of insurance services via Internet.
