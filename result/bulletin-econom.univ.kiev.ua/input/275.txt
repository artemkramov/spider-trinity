Lobova, PhD (Economic), assistant Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv THE RISK INSURANCE EFFICIENCY OF BUSINESS ACTIVITY IN THE AGRICULTURAL SECTOR</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/1540" title="10:28 PM">Sunday November 1st, 2015</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/maxim" title="View all posts by Maxim">Maxim</a></span></span></div><div><p>The necessity of business activity insurance in the agricultural sector is grounded in this article. Different types of agricultural risks are described. The principles,<span></span>on which the efficiency of agricultural insurance is based, are determined. Such as: voluntary; the farmers interest; risk management; multivariate product line; promote farmers efficient; equal access to subsidies from farmers; balance; transparency and coordination of the subsidies transfer; creating a reliable and multi-level system of insurance coverage; binding institute of independent examination. The different ways to determine the effectiveness of insurance are considered.</p><p>Keywords: agricultural insurance; insurance efficiency; estimation of the insurance efficiency.</p><p>DOI:&nbsp;<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2014/156-3/14">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2014/156-3/14</a></p><h5><strong><span>References</span></strong></h5><ol><li>Mizhnarodna finansova korporatsiya: richna zvitnist’ v ahrarnomu sektori [online].
