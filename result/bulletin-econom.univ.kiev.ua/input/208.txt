This process is shown on the example of research of consumers risks at the market of dry wine in Kyiv.</p><p>Keywords: marketing research; consumer risks; wine market.</p><p>DOI:<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/173-8/1">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/173-8/1</a></p><h5><strong>References</strong></h5><ol><li>S.L.
