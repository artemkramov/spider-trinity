Svyatnenko, PhD in Economics, Associate Professor Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine ORGANIZATIONAL SUPPORT OF INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY MANAGEMENT AT THE ENTERPRISE</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/3848" title="11:55 AM">Friday November 27th, 2015</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/admin" title="View all posts by admin">admin</a></span></span></div><div><p>The article deals with theoretical issues of organizational support of intellectual property management at the enterprise.<span></span>It is defined the nature, goals, objectives organization of intellectual property. It is selected the features, factors of influence on the choice of organizational structure and organizational form of intellectual property management on an enterprise. It is proposed systematization forms of organizational maintenance of intellectual property management at different classification criteria. Attention is paid to consider outsourcing forms of operations with intellectual property.</p><p>Keywords: organization, intellectual property, intellectual property management, organizational forms of intellectual property, outsourcing in the management of intellectual property.</p><p>DOI:<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/172-7/6">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/172-7/6</a></p><h5><strong>References</strong></h5><ol><li>Asaul, A.
