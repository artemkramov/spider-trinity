Slukhai, Doctor of Sciences, Professor Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv UKRAINIAN FISCAL EQUALIZATION: DOES IT NEED AN IMPROVEMENT?</h1><div>Posted on<a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/977" title="12:17 PM">Friday October 16th, 2015</a><span>by<span><a href="http://bulletin-econom.univ.kiev.ua/archives/author/admin" title="View all posts by admin">admin</a></span></span></div><div><p>The article states that existing economic and social differences between territories in Ukraine call for use of fiscal equalization instruments. The study shows that implementation of formula-based fiscal equalization has led to a virtually full equalization of differentials in subnational total public revenues and<span></span>expenditures on the regional level. Findings presented give reasons for reviewing a current fiscal equalization procedure with regard to incorporating into equalization grant allocation formula fiscal incentives for subnational governments.</p><p>Keywords: fiscal equalization, intergovernmental transfers, public finance.</p><p>DOI:<a href="http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/166-1/5">http://dx.doi.org/10.17721/1728-2667.2015/166-1/5</a></p><h5><strong>References</strong></h5><p>1.
