D.<br>Astronomical observatory of Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv<br><span></span></p><p><strong>Abstract</strong><br>For the sample of about 800 compact starburst galaxies the luminosity functions are considered based on the data of luminosities of galaxies in the ultraviolet&nbsp; continuum and the recombination&nbsp; Нα emission line in the regions of ionised hydrogen. The luminosity functions of galaxies are described by Saunders function and log-normal distribution. We find the luminosity function parameters for both distributions and show that log-normal distribution provides a good fitting of&nbsp; luminosity functions of galaxy sample. The&nbsp; confidence probability that luminosity function is described by log-nornal distribution is about 27 % accordingly to Fisher criterion. It is better than that described by Schechter function.</p><p><strong>Keywords</strong><br>Compact galaxies</p><p><strong>References</strong><br>Parnovskyi, S.L., Izotova, I.Iu. 2014, Visnyk Kyivskoho universytetu.
