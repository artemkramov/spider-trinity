Fell.<br>State Space Agency of Ukraine<span></span></p><p><strong><span>Abstract<br></span></strong>The operational forecasting service for real-time geomagnetic indices Dst and Kp was described. The warning time for the Earth to the intersection of the Dst index is 1-4 hours, for the Kp index – 3 hours. The skillscore parameter, which is defined as a decrease of the relative mean square error with respect to the trivial model, was approximately 40% for Dst and 15% for Kp. The service works on-line freely available through STAFF http://www.staff.oma.be/ browser.</p><p><strong>Keywords<br></strong>Geomagnetic forecasting</p><p><strong>References<br></strong>Parnowski, A. 2009, Astrophysics &amp; Space Science.,323, 2, 169<br>Parnowski, A.2011, Journal of Physical Studies.,15, 2, 2002<br>Press, W.H., Teukolsky, S.A., Vetterling, W.T., Flannery, B.P. 1992, CambridgeUniv.
