Modern approach to re-processing early observations of Pluto with new technologies and measurements can be an effective treatment for building models of planetary systems and the theory of motion. As a result of combining the efforts of three Ukrainian observatories – the project participants UkrVO – collected, digitized and processed observations of Pluto during 1961–1990 included in the Joint Digital Archive of UkrVO. Using common techniques astronomy negative digitization and further processing for observations obtained 5 telescopes catalogue positions and magnitudes of Pluto.
