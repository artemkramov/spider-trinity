Stashko</strong>, student, Physical Dept.<br>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv<br><span></span><strong>Abstract<br></strong>We study exact special solutions of the joint system of Einstein equations and scalar field equations with a non-zero self-interaction potential, which describe spherically symmetric static configurations. The space-time is asymptotically flat with a naked singularity at the center. The testbody motion is analyzed; we found conditions for existence of non-connected regions of stable circular orbits. We show the existence of static trajectories of particles that hang above the configuration.</p><p><strong>Keywords</strong><br>Gravitational field, scalar field</p><p><strong>References</strong><br>Novosyadlyi, B., Pelykh, V., Shtanov, Yu., Zhuk, A. 2013, Dark energy and dark matter of the universe: in three volumes / Ed.
