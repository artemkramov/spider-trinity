Sociology of Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv)</h3></div><div><em>S. Babenko, L. Males, O. Kharchenko, T. Chervinska</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>The article is focused on the results and the outcomes of the gender audit conducted at the Faculty of Sociology Taras Shevchenko National&nbsp;University of Kyiv in September-October 2015 within the frame of the project "Gender Mainstreaming in Universities of Ukraine". This project&nbsp;involved a participative gender audit in 15 Universities of Ukraine on the basis of the methodology and procedures developed by the International&nbsp;Labor Organization (ILO) in order to assess the achievements of organizations in terms of gender equality. The study assessed the relevance of&nbsp;gender equality for education and sociological education in particular, gender stereotypes spread in professional and private spheres, especially&nbsp;the gender bias in education and the labor market. Also some recommendations to improve the situation in terms of the proceeding gender equality&nbsp;are formulated. Gender audit is proposed as a research strategy to provide a feedback to the reforms in Universities and higher education. In&nbsp;particular reforms of educational organizations, social relations among students, professorship, administration at universities, relevance change of&nbsp;gender bias in education and labor market on the way to gender equality.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>gender equality; gender audit; gender bias; gender stereotype; gender sensitivity</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/121/97">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Закон України Про забезпечення рівних прав та можливостей жінок і чоловіків, 2003.
