href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/57/0">Sporysh</a></div><div><div></div><div><h3>Leisure Practices of Urban Middle Classes in Ukraine</h3></div><div><em>Y. Sporysh</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div>The article deal with study of features of the leisure practices of representatives of the middle class, living in large cities. As the empirical database we used the results of sociological survey "Omnibus 2006" conducted by the Institute of Sociology of NAS of Ukraine in 2006. It is proved that the leisure practices of middle-class differ from the leisure practices of other social groups in Ukrainian society by a greater prevalence of actions to reproduce and increase the intellectual and cultural capital. In particular, these differences are emerge in the regular reading of fiction, newspapers and magazines, have been using a computer and internet for training, the use of active forms of recreation, self-organized education, etc.</div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>leisure; life style; middle class; the intellectual and cultural capital</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/57/42">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Bendat Dzh.
