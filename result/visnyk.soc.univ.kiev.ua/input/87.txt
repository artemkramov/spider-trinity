Students’ Civil Identity</h3></div><div><em>L. Sokuryanska</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>The article presents the findings of empirical analysis of the factors that are involved in construction of Ukrainian students' civic identity. In particular, three axes of such factors (institutional, resource and practical) are considered. On the basis of empirical analysis of such individual resources as cultural capital and personal value orientations, it is proved that the most intense axis of civil identity construction is the practical axis.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>civil identity; axis of factors of civic identity construction; cultural capital; value orientations</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/105/81">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Makeev S.A.
