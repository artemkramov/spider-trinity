Strelnyk</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>The article deals with the problem of commodification of motherhood on the example of modern reproductive technologies and pronatalist&nbsp;discourse as a socio-cultural basis of their market development. The author notes that ART, on the one hand, help infertile couples to realize the&nbsp;right to parenting. On the other hand, the fact of concentration of such services in commercial sector of medicine amends into the access to such&nbsp;services and their control.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>commodification; auxiliary reproductive technologies (ART); motherhood; fatherhood; pronatalist discourse; medicalization.</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/38/24">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Arendell T.
