href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/70/0">Obuhova</a></div><div><div></div><div><h3>Structure of Symbolic Space in а City's Image (in the case of Donetsk)</h3></div><div><em>N. Obuhova</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div>The results of studies of perception of the image of the city by its inhabitants are presented on the example of Donetsk city. "The city's image" is defined as a structure of description of a city and expression of group consciousness of the city's inhabitants. The image of Donetsk is studied through the structure of symbolic space, presented by the monuments as well as the perception of their importance. There are singled out "cultural vacuum" and emphasized the importance of creative cultural space of the city development.</div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>city image; cultural space; city's symbolic space; postindustrialisation.</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/70/53">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Gry`czak Ya.
