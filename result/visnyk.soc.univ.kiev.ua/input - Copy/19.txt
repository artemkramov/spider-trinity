person</h3></div><div><em>І. Nabrusko</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>This article analyzes the main approaches to the study of food practices in sociological tradition. The attention is paid to the imperative &nbsp;dimension of gastronomic practices, where they come outas the social way to influence people. Their creation, despite theexistentialnature, &nbsp;depends almost entirely onexistingculturalnorms. Some trends of modern gastronomic practices are considered, where their total character is focused on a set of almost all social positions, the growth of semantic-symbolic component when food gets extra meanings and values,diffuse essenceand industrial nature, organized on the principles of rationality specialized production, are particularly evident. Such tendencies lead to new ways of social adaptation of modern society. The article is an attempt to provide the classification of main gastronomic practices of modern &nbsp;society, which offers four main discursive approaches: culinary, status, health and national discourse, in the framework of which food practices of &nbsp;modern man are formed. These modern practices on the one hand act as the need to ensure internal social harmony and identification, and on the &nbsp;other as an expression of social pressures external cultural norms.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>everyday life; gastronomic practices; socio-cultural nature of food</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/112/88">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Бергер П., Лукман Т.
