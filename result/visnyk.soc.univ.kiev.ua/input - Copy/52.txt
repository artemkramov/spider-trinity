href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/76/0">Fitisova</a></div><div><div></div><div><h3>STUDENT'S REPRESENTATIONS OF FACULTY'S DEPARTMENTS: TOWARDS PHENOMENOLOGICAL ANALYSIS OF SIGNIFICANT MEANING STRUCTURES IN HIGHER EDUCATION</h3></div><div><em>A. Fitisova, A. Deineka</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><br><p>The article presents results of original phenomenological research, which devoted to description and interpretation of intersubjective field of meanings that exist at the Faculty of Sociology, Taras Shevchenko National University of Kiev. Authors analyze typical understandings and views of students regarding the faculty departments, by using qualitative dataset, composed of depth-interview transcripts, open non-structured questionnaires and materials of some projective tests. The paper proposes to go beyond the traditional logic of functionalist analysis of education and to do some theoretical generalizations and problematizations by actualization of phenomenological cognitive strategies.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Keywords</h4><br><div>phenomenology; sociology of education; intersubjective space; faculty of Sociology; image of faculty department; relevancy system; biographical situation; unit of experience; sedimentation; social action</div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://visnyk.soc.univ.kiev.ua/index.php/soc/article/view/76/56">PDF (Українська)</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Bourdieu P., Passeron J-C., La reproduction, Éléments d'une théorie du système d'enseignement per. s fr. – M.
