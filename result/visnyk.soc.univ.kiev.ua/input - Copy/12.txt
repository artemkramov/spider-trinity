Zakharenko</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div>&nbsp;<em><strong>This article determines category "value code" in modern science based on concepts of M. Weber, E. Durkheim, J. Dewey, R. Highlights mechanisms of representation of value codes in modern Ukrainian films production time of independent Ukraine (1991-2016). Analyzes the modern Ukrainian movies on filling their value codes based on sociocultural model of basic values of the Soviet sociologist M.
