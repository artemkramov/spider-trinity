SIGNAL</h3></div><div><em>Vladislav Noskov, Kirill Ignatkov, Sergey Smolskiy</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>Research results on autodyne oscillator stabilized by an external high-Q cavity are presented for the case of the large reflected&nbsp;signal, when amplitude of the reflected wave is commensurable with amplitude of natural oscillations. Expressions describing&nbsp;an autodyne response of the oscillator on the influence of the proper radiation reflected from a target are obtained. Calculations&nbsp;of amplitude, frequency and spectral characteristics of the autodyne system are performed. Conditions of exact tuning of the&nbsp;stabilized cavity are determined. Investigations of stabilized autodyne parameters at small distance to the reflected object are&nbsp;provided. It is shown that to expand the dynamic range of the autodyne system it is expedient to use the large coupling between&nbsp;cavities. Recommendations concerning practical application of the obtained results in the short-range radar are given.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30063/26791">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Page C.
