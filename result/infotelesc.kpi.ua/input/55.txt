problems of mobile communication networks are&nbsp;considered. The article is aimed to solve the relevant scientific and applied problem of planning and optimization of cellular&nbsp;mobile communication networks along with taking into account the totality quality indices, based on the application of multicriterial&nbsp;optimization methods.
