EMERGENCY</h3></div><div><em>Oleksandr Lysenko, Stanislav Valuiskyi, Pavel Kirchu, Anton Romaniuk</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>This paper addresses to a method for increasing of mobile ad-hoc networks throughput based on the placement control of unmanned&nbsp;aerial vehicles in the area of emergency situation. A further development of this method, namely the improvement of&nbsp;UAV flight control subsystem, that will allow operative implementation of obtained in the previous stage location coordinates&nbsp;while minimizing energy consumption for control, is proposed. The proposed approach will improve network throughput by&nbsp;15–20% while reducing fuel costs by an average of 13–15%.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30221/26949">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Bunin S.G., Voiter A.P., Ilchenko M.Y., Romaniuk V.А.
