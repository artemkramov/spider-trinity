VALIDATION</h3></div><div><em>Volodymyr Kononenko, Serhii Kravchuk</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>In the paper a multilevel intellectual approach to HTTP-requests legitimacy validation is proposed. The approach is devised for&nbsp;HTTP-flood DDoS-attacks detection and prevention in telecommunication networks with a web-server as the target attack object.&nbsp;The analysis of HTTP-requests attributes and their signatures is provided. On the basis of the analysis the attributes are&nbsp;separated into several levels that allow us to design a flow analyzer in a form of the multilevel block. Due to a multilevel structure&nbsp;of the flow analyzer a minimization of resources, spent for a request handling, is achieved.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30223/26951">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>RFC4732: Internet Denial-of-Service Considerations: http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc4732</p><p>RFC2616: Hypertext Transfer Protocol – HTTP/1.1: http://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc2616</p><p>Kononenko V.M.
