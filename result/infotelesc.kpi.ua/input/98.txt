TRANSISTORS</h3></div><div><em>Vladimir Lyubchenko, Yevgeniy Yunevich, Valeriy Kalinin, Viktor Kotov, Dmitriy Radchenko, Sergey Telegin</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>Electromagnetic wave generation processes in the hybrid log-periodic microstripe antenna on the dielectric substrate integrated&nbsp;with field-effect transistor are experimentally studied in the frequency range of 6─20 GHz. The possibility of synchronization&nbsp;and power combining in the array consisting of antenna-coupled oscillators placed on the common dielectric substrate is investigated.&nbsp;It is shown that the considerable increasing the generation efficiency and power combining can be obtained by using&nbsp;the external synchronization signal or quasi-optical design of the array.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30201/26912">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Lyubchenko V.
