MODULATION</h3></div><div><em>Teodor Narytnyk</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>The telecast and Internet distribution system, which use combined two-level modulation and intended for digital information&nbsp;transfer in frequency band 11,7─12,5 GHz was created on a base of the existing microwave integrated telecommunication system&nbsp;MІTRІS. In comparison with telecommunication system using 64-QAM modulation with one carrier, new system has significantly&nbsp;better power engineering, less hard requirements to the frequency stability, to the phase noise level and to the linearity&nbsp;of the amplitude-frequency and phase-frequency characteristics of communication channel.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30059/26788">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Krivosheev M.
