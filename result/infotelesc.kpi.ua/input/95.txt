TECHNIQUE</h3></div><div><em>Alexander Sychev, Mikhail Chekalin, Vasily Shestakov</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>Computer aided microstrip structures modeling has been performed by conformal mapping technique. A new approach to reduce&nbsp;the connectivity order of the original cross-section geometry of the structure is proposed. The multiply connected domain<br>is reduced to simply connected ones by implementation of magnetic slits concept. The microstrip structures analysis is carried&nbsp;out by numerical conformal transformations technique realized in Schwarz–Christoffel toolbox for MATLAB. This technique&nbsp;is applied to the quasi-static analysis of coupled microstrip lines taking into account the conductor thickness. Described approach&nbsp;ensures high numerical efficiency and can be used for accurate analysis of complex microstrip structures.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30198/26909">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Field modeling of a multiconductor digital bus / H.
