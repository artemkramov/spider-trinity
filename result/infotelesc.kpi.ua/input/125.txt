(CriMiCo)</h3></div><div><em>Mykhaylo Ilchenko, Pavlo Yermolov</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>The International Crimean Microwave Conference (CriMiCo) is convened in Sevastopol since 1991. 20 years saw that this&nbsp;conference became the flagship venue for far reaching research into theoretical, experimental, manufacturing, applied and historical&nbsp;aspects of microwave engineering and telecommunication technology. Solely in 2010 over 500 reports were delivered&nbsp;by scientists and experts from 208 universities and 17 countries: Belarus, Belgium, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Germany, Israel,&nbsp;Iran, Kazakhstan, Canada, China, Korea, Lithuania, Moldova, Poland, Russia, USA, Ukraine and the Republic of South Africa.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30011/26743">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>[Letter from J.
