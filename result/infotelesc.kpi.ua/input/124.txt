Prokopenko, Yuriy Poplavko, Vitaliy Molchanov</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>High-tunable low-loss microwave dielectric devices are designed by electromechanical alteration in the configuration of device&nbsp;components. Specifically, this device consists of dielectric and metallic parts with an air gap between them. The gap location&nbsp;provides the largest perturbation in the electromagnetic field and a mini-actuator controls the device. Through experiments&nbsp;conducted, we test and apply the high-tunable dielectric resonators and filters as well as wide-band low loss phase shifters.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30006/26740">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>Lucyszyn S. and Robertson I.
