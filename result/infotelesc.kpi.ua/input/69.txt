characteristics research is&nbsp;developed. The OFDM signal vector-phase states are examined. The momentary amplitudes distribution function is obtained.&nbsp;A hypothesis of amplitude distribution law is verified by the X2 criterion. The OFDM subcarriers optimal gain method is&nbsp;proposed.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/30231/26958">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>R.
