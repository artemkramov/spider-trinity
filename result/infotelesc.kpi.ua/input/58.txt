CAPABILITIES</h3></div><div><em>Leonid O. Uryvsky, Serhii O. Osypchuk</em></div><br><div><h4>Abstract</h4><br><div><p>The error-correcting capabilities of regular LDPC (Low Density Parity Check) codes and BCH (Bose-Chaudhuri-Hocquenguem) codes are examined. The qualitative analysis and the quantitative assessment of error-correcting abilities are&nbsp;performed for LDPC codes with code word length n=1000 bits and BCH codes with code word length n=1023 bits. The code&nbsp;rates of LDPC and BCH codes are determined for a known signal to noise ratio in the gaussian channel; detected code rates are&nbsp;optimal for predefined modulation type and required information reliability on the receiver side.</p></div><br></div><div><h4>Full Text:</h4><a href="http://infotelesc.kpi.ua/article/view/28569/25549">PDF</a></div><div><h4>References</h4><br><div><p>T.
