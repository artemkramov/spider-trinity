Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>The article deals with theoretical and applied principles of forming the cost of housing the regional centers of the Western region. The dependence of housing costs on the socio-economic situation of the region was determinated. The authors analyzed the value of housing within individual cities and their parts. The estimation of indicators of settlement, population, territory provisions which have a direct impact on the value of the area.
