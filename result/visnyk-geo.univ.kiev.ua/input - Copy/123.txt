VALUES</strong></p><p>Getman V.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Кyiv, Кyiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>On the Ukraine’s territory, as well as elsewhere, there are not only unique, picturesque places but also energetically powerful ones, whose phenomenon is deeply rooted in the past, i.e., their natural diversity interacts with cultural and historical wealth. Among those places is Posullia, where a national nature park was created. This park, ways of natural biodiversity protection, and spiritual power of this land are discussed in this article.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>national nature park, biological diversity, recreational resources</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:&nbsp;</strong><a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.6">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.6</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Het’man V.I.
