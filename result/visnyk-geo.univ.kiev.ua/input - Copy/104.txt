CAPABILITY</strong></p><p>Ivah Ya., Senkiv M.</p><p>Ivan Franko National University of Lviv, Lviv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>Ukraine’s transport infrastructure, in particular, railway, motor, aviation and maritime, is analyzed from the point of view of military logistics. The influence of transport geologistics, and in particular transport infrastructure development on Ukraine’s defence capability level in the modern war conditions is substantiated. Based on the graph theory the estimation of transport and geographical position of motor transport junctions on the major highways, as an important factor of transport geologistics in Ukraine, is made. The ways of increasing the level of Ukraine’s defense capability in the context of transport geologistics are proposed.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>transport infrastructure, transport network, transport and geographical position, transport geologistics, military logistics, state defence capability</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:</strong>&nbsp;<a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2015.63.10">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2015.63.10</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Hrytsevych V., Sen’chuk Kh.
