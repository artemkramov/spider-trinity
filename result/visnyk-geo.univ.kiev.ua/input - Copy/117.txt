SHORT SUMMER HOLIDAYS</strong></p><p>Anisimov S.</p><p>Research establishment “Ukrainian Scientific Research Institute of Ecological Problems “, Kharkiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>Analysis of demand for recreational services carried out on the basis of the literature data and the results of a questionnaire survey. Motivations, expectations and interests of unorganized tourists on the coast of the S. Donets River in the summer of 2013-2014 was studied. Territorial preferences, the structure and the length of recreational activities have been identified. The characteristics of desirable indicators of natural-resource potential of the territory and economic infrastructure has been received.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>recreational services, questionnaire survey, territorial preferences of holidaymakers, structure and duration of the recreational activities</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:</strong>&nbsp;<a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2015.63.23">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2015.63.23</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Byrkovych V.
