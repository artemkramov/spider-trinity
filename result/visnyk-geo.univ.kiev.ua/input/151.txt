PROCESSES</strong></p><p>Kliuiko T.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>The current state of urbanization processes in the world Disclosed the concept of urbanization within a narrow and broad sense, identified the main factors of urban dissemination.
