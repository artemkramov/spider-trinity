ASPECTS</strong></p><p>Stebletska Yu.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>Considered the essence of the concept “master plan” of cities, its structure and role in urban development. Distinguished historical and urban planning stages of planning of spatial development of Kyiv. In detail analyzed master plans of Kyiv in every historical and urban planning stage. Determined the main characteristic features of master plans of Kyiv in every historical and urban planning stage. Explored changing of ideas about the “capital city”. Generalized the processes of spatial development of Kyiv. Determined the prevailing scheme of city planning embedded in the master plans in the context of historical and urban planning stages.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>master plan of a city, planning structure, functional zoning</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:</strong>&nbsp;<a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.17">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.17</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Bilokon’ Yu.
