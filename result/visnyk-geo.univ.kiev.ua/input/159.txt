DISTRICTS OF THE REGION INVESTIGATED</strong></p><p>Nesterchuk I.</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>Strategic tasks of touching optimization of the natural using in the geoecological areas of Shytomyr region are revealed in this article. The theoretical and methodological aspects of geoecological analysis of nature management at the regional level (scientific approaches, base propositions, concepts) involving methodological approaches to geoecological zoning are described. The algorithm of geoecological researches in Zhytomyr region is suggested.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>geoecological analysis, geoecological micfrodistrict, nature management, antropogenic transformation, geoecological potential, technogenic load, geoecological problems</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><p>1.
