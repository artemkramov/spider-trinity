TRAVEL GEOGRAPHY: UKRAINIAN EXPERIENCE</strong></p><p>Smyrnov I.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Кyiv, Кyiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>Shown use of military logistics as a tourist resource in composition of travel geography. Disclosed the peculiarities of the Ukrainian experience in the context of the use of military logistics as a tourist resource as part of the geography of tourism. Cleared out the features of military logistics on example of Ukrainian Cossacks, Battle under Kruty, the Arpad line. Researched use of the above features of military logistics experience as Ukrainian tourism resources in geography of tourism and development of tourism practice in Ukraine.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>military logistics, tourism resources, tourism geography, Ukrainian experience, tourist activities, military and historical tourism, nostalgic tourism</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:&nbsp;</strong><a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.2">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.2</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Zinkevych O., Zinkevych N.
