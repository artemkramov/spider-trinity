PARK</strong></p><p>Getman V.</p><p>Taras Shevchenko National University of Кyiv, Кyiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>There have been raised several questions about saving natural, historical and cultural values of the territory of national park “Dnister river canyon” in this article. Also, this work highlights historical and natural conditions of canyon’s formation. Landscape, aesthetic and recreational resources of Dnister river canyon are incomparable among the territory of Ukraine. That is why it is important to save it from massive anthropogenic pressure. On of the stresses is a planned construction of upper Dnister river cascade hydropower station on the protected territory of national park “Dnister river canyon”.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>national park, landscape and recreational resources, flora, fauna</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:&nbsp;</strong><a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2016.64.4">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2016.64.4</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Arheologiya Ukrainskoj SSR.
