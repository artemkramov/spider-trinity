IMPACT ON THE TOURISM AREA IN THE REGION</strong></p><p><sup>1</sup>Ulyhanets S.,<sup>1</sup>Melnyk L.,<sup>2</sup>Feshchenko V.</p><p><sup>1</sup>Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Kyiv, Ukraine</p><p><sup>2</sup>Protection Department of Cabinet of Ministers’ Secretariat, Kyiv, Ukraine</p><p><strong>Abstract:</strong></p><p>The article highlights the ecological status of environmental components of Transcarpathia and their impact on the tourism industry in the region .In this article the effect of the economic activities of individual industrial enterprises and the environment are investigated, the main solutions to existing environmental problems in the region related to environmental management are deterenvironmentmined. Proposals as for improving the strategy in making ecology-oriented solutions to minimize anthropogenic-technogenic impact of industry on the Transcarpathian region environment are formulated.</p><p><strong>Keywords:</strong></p><p>environment, a tourist area, environmental protection, the current state</p><p><strong>Language:</strong></p><p>ukrainian</p><p><strong>DOI:</strong>&nbsp;<a href="http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.8">http://doi.org/10.17721/1728-2721.2014.62.8</a></p><p><strong>References:</strong></p><ol><li>Aharkova N.V., Kachyns’kyy A.B., Stepanenko A.V.
